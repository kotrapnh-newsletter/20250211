Doosan robots in Thailand
Doosan Robotics said Wednesday it is ramping up its market expansion in Thailand, forging a deal to supply 300 collaborative robots with Bangkok-based VRNJ, a robot systems supplier. The robots include the A, H, M and P series, which ensure a high level of safety and precision work, handling a payload of up to 30 kilograms in logistics applications. →
S. Korea ranks 1st in global shipbuilding orders in Jan.
South Korea ranked first in new global shipbuilding orders in January, outpacing its rival China, industry data showed Friday. South Korean shipyards clinched orders totaling 900,000 compensated gross tons for 13 ships, accounting for 62 percent of the global total at 1.46 million CGTs last month. →
S. Korea, Vietnam hold talks on ways to expand economic cooperation
South Korea and Vietnam held talks Friday to discuss ways to expand bilateral trade and cooperation in the supply chain and energy industries, the industry ministry said. Senior officials of the countries discussed various topics, including the new US administration, global supply chain, and promoting cooperation in the nuclear power plant industry and other areas. →
